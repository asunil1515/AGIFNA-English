About the Conference

The Reserve Conference is a four-day gathering rooted in Jesus' teaching in Matthew 25:1–13 — the parable of the ten virgins.

All ten had lamps. All ten were waiting. But only five carried reserve oil. The heart of this conference is a call to preserve and cultivate the oil: the anointing, intimacy, and ongoing relationship with the Holy Spirit, which cannot be borrowed or manufactured in moments of need.

This conference is not about hype, emotionalism, or momentary encounters alone. It is about forming believers who walk with sustained oil.
